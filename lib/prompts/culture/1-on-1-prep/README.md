# 1-on-1 Pre-Read Email Generator

Generates structured pre-read emails for 1-on-1 meetings enabling effective async communication.

## What You'll Be Asked

- **Time period** - Week, two weeks, or month being covered
- **Your name and role** - Team member name and title
- **Manager name** - Your manager's name
- **Current projects** (optional) - Focus areas and active work
- **Team context** (optional) - Upcoming launches, team changes
