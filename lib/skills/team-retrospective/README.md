# Team Retrospective Facilitator

Generates structured retrospective agendas, facilitates team reflection on practices and outcomes, and produces actionable improvement plans. Supports multiple formats including Start/Stop/Continue, 4Ls, Sailboat, Mad/Sad/Glad, and Starfish. Adapts facilitation approach to team sentiment, producing materials with prioritized action items, team health checks, and follow-up checklists.

## What You'll Be Asked

- **Scope**: Sprint, project, incident, or quarterly reflection
- **Format**: Start/Stop/Continue, 4Ls, Sailboat, Mad/Sad/Glad, Starfish, or suggested
- **Team sentiment**: Positive, mixed, frustrated, or neutral

## Usage Examples

### Sprint Start/Stop/Continue

> "Run a retro for our sprint. We shipped checkout redesign but had deployment issues."

Produces a Start/Stop/Continue retrospective with tables for practices to adopt, eliminate, and continue, plus prioritized action items and follow-up checklist.

### Project 4Ls Retrospective

> "We just launched our mobile app after 3 months. Team is tired but proud. Use 4Ls format."

Generates a 4Ls retrospective covering what went well, lessons learned, missing elements, and future wishes, with quantitative reflection and an appreciation circle.

### Sailboat Migration Retro

> "Sailboat retro for our platform team migrating to Kubernetes. Progress is slow."

Creates a Sailboat retrospective with tailwinds, weighted anchors, risk matrix for rocks ahead, and island definition of done, including a morale check-in.

## Key Features

- Five retrospective formats with automatic format recommendation
- Prioritized action items limited to 3-5 for focus and follow-through
- Team health check pulse survey for ongoing sentiment tracking
- Previous action item follow-up for accountability
- Psychological safety framing built into facilitation materials
- Appreciation and recognition prompts to end on a positive note

## Installation

```bash
hit install team-retrospective
```
