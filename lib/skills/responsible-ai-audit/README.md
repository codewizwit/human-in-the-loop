# Responsible AI Audit

Evaluates AI systems and outputs against responsible AI principles including accuracy, fairness, transparency, accountability, safety, and privacy. Produces a structured audit report with lens-by-lens analysis, risk assessment, mitigation recommendations, and a deployment decision framework.

## What You'll Be Asked

- **System type**: AI-powered tool, specific AI output, or AI/ML pipeline
- **Purpose**: Assists users, automates decisions, generates content, or other
- **Risk level**: High (impacts people/safety), Medium (human-reviewed), or Low (informational)
- **Known concerns**: Bias/fairness, transparency, safety, or full audit

## Usage Examples

### AI Tool Audit

> "Audit this AI code review bot that auto-comments on PRs"

Produces a comprehensive audit evaluating the tool across all accountability lenses with a deployment recommendation and mitigation plan.

### AI Output Fairness Review

> "Review our AI content moderation pipeline for bias and fairness"

Generates a focused audit on fairness and bias detection across content categories and user demographics.

### Pre-Deployment Review

> "Evaluate our AI test generator for responsible AI compliance before launch"

Creates a deployment readiness assessment with conditions, monitoring plan, and success criteria.
